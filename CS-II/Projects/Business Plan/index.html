SSS Search

This is the greatest, well-built, and the most secure search engine the world has ever seen.

Identity

This search engine will provide the users with the best industry-leading features leveraging cutting edge technologies that empowers the users and puts them first.

Problem

Users are looking for new and innovative search engines that suit their daily needs with personalized features tailored to them according to their usage.

Our Solution

The search engine provides the uterrmost respect to user’s privacy without annoying ads and a beautiful design that attracts consumers. It helps with everyday tasks such as shopping, research (for school and work), and Even better, it gives the most accurate and comprehensive search results than any of the leading competitors.

Target Market

The target market is for people all over the world who are using the Internet that are annoyed with the old and slow search engines.

Competition

The biggest competitions in the market are Google and Microsoft Bing, and Yahoo. Google Search was officially launched in 1998 by Larry Page and Sergey Brin to which has become the most widely used web-based search engine. With over 5.4 billion searches each day and over 90% market share, Google dominates the industry since the web became accessible to the public with its rivals lagging far behind. Microsoft Bing originating from past ventures by Microsoft like MSN Search, Windows Live Search, and Windows Search has about 8%-9% of market share leading it to be the second most used search engine in the world. Yahoo trails behind Google and Microsoft with about 2% market share and it uses similar technology that of Microsoft Bing because the both struck a deal in 2009 which provided that Bing would power Yahoo’s search engine.

Revenue Streams

Most of the revenue will be driven from pay-per-click ads which will be generated at the top of the webpage after a search, labeled as “AD” at the beginning, to let users know that the result was paid by the organization. Pay-per-click advertising is where companies bid on certain keywords that the users are likely to type pertaining to their industry. Then, each time the user clicks on the ad, that company has to pay a certain amount of money to the search engine as it generates traffic for the website. Some of the revenue will be generated from public donations. There will be a very small banner at the top of the search engine notifying the users that this search engine has been created by a small group of hardworking individuals that strive to keep the web safe, secure, and speedy for all people.

Marketing Activities

Main marketing events will be through social media platforms, Twitter, Linkedin, Facebook, and Instagram. Tweets, and messages will be sent out daily through these platforms to promote the use of the search engine. Also, colorful posters will be made detailing the important features that are embedded in the search engine enticing the users that it is the best and most powerful search tool. Furthermore, advertisements will be placed on Youtube, Linkedin, and on mainstream websites. SSS Search will look for and hire people who have good voices for voice overs in video ads which could compel the audience to try the new search engine.

Team and Key Roles

  • Project Manager
    • The project manager will coordinate all the tasks and communicate the tasks that need to be done to each of the members. Also, the manager will work with each person to ensure the work is being sufficiently complemented.
  • Programmers (2)
    • Creating the algorithm for the search engine to sort and filter out the best search results according to the keywords. Furthermore, they need to implement and maintain the features and components of a search engine
  • Backend Development
    • Maintaining, updating, and monitoring the backend systems such as AWS and Firebase. Also, they will be managing all the databases related to search queries. Furthermore, the member will be tasked with identifying and fixing security vulnerabilities.
  • Marketing/Graphic Manager
    • Tasks : Creating/ maintaining media that promotes the SSS Search through platforms such as: flyers/ posters, videos, social media, and video ads. Also, the member will be tasked with creating the design for the search engine.
