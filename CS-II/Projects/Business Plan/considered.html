But, the reason for rejecting the proposal is that using AI to detect babies could lead to ethical issues. For example. Google Photos became controversial in the news when its was applying innaporitate naming conventions to colored people. ShelterSafe

The proposal is very interesting because it attempts to solve a problem that many government are battling to figure out during emergency situations like during hurricanes and tornadoes. Furthermore, governments are worried that people are not prepared and important information about shelters and food is not being sent properly. The reason for rejecting this proposal is that the program heavily relies on Apple iOS phones to communicate each other. It would provide an obstacle to many consumers as not all people use Apple smartphones. It should include features from all types of devices (ex: tablets, Android smartphones).

MyCasino

I like this project as it adds a spin and creativity to a “casino game”. The creator Jake Podewils has said that in order for people to level up in the game, they have to solve hard math and science problems. But, operating a casino game could to lead to tax regulations, and legal implications put forth by government. The proposal does not include how money would be handled safely. As a casino game operator, they need to have high security standards in their program to deter hackers and criminals away but this is not present in the documentation provided the creator.
